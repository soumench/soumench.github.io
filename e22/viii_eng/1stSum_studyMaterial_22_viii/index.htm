4. Change the voice:.
(i) He has made a kite.   ( Answer: A kite has been made by him.
)
(ii) A picture was drawn by my brother.    ( Answer: My brother drew a picture.)
(iii) They pulled the fairy cap.    ( Answer: The fairy cap was pulled by them.)
(iv) He saw the rain.   ( Answer: The rain was seen by him.)
5. Change the mode of narration:
(i) He said to me,”Please give me a pen”. [to indirect ] ( Answer: He requested me to give him a pen.)
(ii) Grandfather wished me that I might be happy. [to direct]      ( Answer: Grandfather said to me,”May you be happy.”)
(iii) “No good will come of the wind cap”, she said. [to indirect ]         ( Answer: She told worrisomely that no good would come of the wind cap.)
(iv) The old man said,“It is not enough for the cloud to come.” [to indirect ]         ( Answer: The old man told that it was not enough for the cloud to go.)
